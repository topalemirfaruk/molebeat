Why MoleBeat?

Dynamic Albums

Automatically organizes your library by album and artist for a clean browsing experience.

Professional EQ

10-band equalizer with built-in presets like Bass Boost, Rock, and Pop.

Theme Engine

Customize the look with 6 vibrant accent colors to match your style.

Drag & Drop

Simply drag your music files into the app to instantly add them to your library.

Mini Player

Keep your music on top with a compact, picture-in-picture mode.

Synced Lyrics

Sing along with synchronized lyrics support (LRC) for your favorite tracks.
